About

I am a physicist and research software engineer bridging the gap between theoretical quantum dynamics and scalable hardware implementation. My research focuses on developing resource-efficient algorithms that push the boundaries of NISQ capabilities. With a strong foundation in Tensor Network theory and Circuit Compilation, I build tools that make quantum dynamics simulatable and robust against noise. I am driven by the challenge of scaling quantum advantages to solve real-world computational problems at the intersection of HPC and Quantum.
